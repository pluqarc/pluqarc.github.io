Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 27, 2025

Developer Information

This privacy policy applies to the Maze game ("Game" or "App") developed and published by Pluq Arc, an independent developer based in Tunisia.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Game available through the Google Play Store.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Information Collected Through Google Play Games Services

When you connect to Google Play Games Services to save your game progress, the following information may be collected:

  • Your Google Play Games username
  • Player ID (a unique identifier assigned by Google Play Games)
  • Game progress data (levels completed, current progress)

This information is collected and managed by Google Play Games Services. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information about how Google handles this data.

1.2 Information Collected for Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our Game. AdMob may collect the following information:

  • Advertising ID (GAID - Google Advertising Identity)
  • Device information (device type, operating system, language)
  • IP address (for approximate geographic location)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

Advertising ID: This is a unique, non-permanent identifier for your device used for advertising purposes. You can reset or opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings (see section 4.2 below).

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do NOT collect:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not collect precise location data
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, or contacts
  • We do not collect any personal information directly

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Game Progress: To save and sync your game progress across devices through Google Play Games Services
  • Advertisements: To display ads in the Game and provide you with a free gaming experience
  • Personalized Advertising: With your consent (for users in the European Economic Area), to show you more relevant advertisements based on your interests
  • In-App Purchases: To process your purchases through the Google Play Store

3. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

We share information with the following third-party service providers:

3.1 Google Play Games Services

We use Google Play Games Services to enable cloud saving of your game progress. Information shared includes your player ID, username, and game progress data.

Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob collects and processes advertising data as described in section 1.2.

Privacy Policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/7665968

3.3 No Other Third Parties

We do not share your information with any other third parties, partners, or service providers.

4. ADVERTISING

4.1 In-Game Advertising

We display advertisements in our Game through Google AdMob to provide the Game free of charge. These ads help us maintain and improve the Game.

4.2 Personalized Advertising and Consent

For Users in the European Economic Area (EEA):

Before showing personalized advertisements, we will ask for your consent. You will see a consent dialog when you first launch the Game. You can choose to:

  • Accept: You will see personalized ads based on your interests
  • Reject: You will see non-personalized ads that are not based on your interests

You can change your choice at any time through the Game settings.

The legal basis for showing personalized ads to EEA users is consent in accordance with GDPR Article 6(1)(a).

4.3 How to Turn Off Personalized Ads

You can control personalized advertising through your device settings:

Android:

  1. Open the "Settings" application
  2. Select "Google" (you may need to use the search function)
  3. Select "Ads"
  4. Activate "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  5. You can also reset your advertising ID in the same menu

4.4 Removing Advertisements

You can make a purchase to remove advertisements from the Game. This purchase is processed through Google Play Store, and we do not have access to your payment information. All payment transactions are handled by Google.

5. GOOGLE PLAY GAMES SERVICES

Our Game uses Google Play Games Services to enable cloud saving of your game progress. When you connect to Google Play Games:

  • You will be asked to sign in with your Google account
  • Google Play Games will collect your username, player ID, and game progress
  • Your progress will be synchronized across devices where you're signed in
  • We only store the minimum information necessary to identify your saved progress

Google Play Games Services is governed by Google's own privacy policy. For more information, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. IN-APP PURCHASES

The Game includes optional in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled by the Google Play Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or any billing data.

All purchase transactions are managed through your Google Play account and are governed by Google Play's terms and policies.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our Game is suitable for all ages, including children. We are committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • We do not require users to provide any personal information to play the Game
  • All data collection is handled by Google Play Games Services and AdMob, which have their own child protection measures

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at pluqarc@gmail.com and we will take steps to remove such information.

8. YOUR RIGHTS (For EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You can request information about the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent for personalized advertising at any time through the Game settings
  • Right to Object: You can object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a structured format
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You can file a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at pluqarc@gmail.com

9. DATA SECURITY

We take the security of your information seriously. However, since we collect minimal information and rely on third-party services (Google Play Games and AdMob), the security of your data is primarily managed by these services, which implement industry-standard security measures.

10. DATA RETENTION

We retain information only as long as necessary:

  • Game Progress: Stored by Google Play Games Services as long as you maintain your account
  • Advertising Data: Managed by Google AdMob according to their retention policies

You can delete your game progress by disconnecting from Google Play Games Services or deleting your Google Play Games data.

11. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States where Google's servers are located. These countries may have different data protection laws than your country.

Google implements appropriate safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses, to protect your information when it is transferred internationally.

The most current version of this Privacy Policy will always be available at: https://pluqarc.github.io/privacy-policy.html
